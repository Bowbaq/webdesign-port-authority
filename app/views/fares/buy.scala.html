Port Authority Downtown Service Center


Our Downtown Service Center sells passes and tickets, and provides hand schedules and general service information, as well as applications for half-fare cards and Pennsylvania Senior Citizen ID cards.


Downtown Service Center
412-255-1356
534 Smithfield Street, Downtown Pittsburgh


DaysHours
Monday through Friday8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
Weekends and HolidaysCLOSED


Our Downtown Service Center accepts the following forms of payment:


  • Cash
  • Credit cards: MasterCard, Visa, Discover and American Express
  • Debit cards with a MasterCard, Visa, Discover or American Express logo. Please note that these are processed like credit cards without PIN entry. We do not offer cash back from debit transactions.
  • Money orders
  • Corporate checks
  • Personal checks are only accepted on mail-in orders for annual pass subscriptions. For questions, call 412-255-1356.
